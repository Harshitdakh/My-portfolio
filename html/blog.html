Delicious Dessert Recipe

A quick guide to making a mouthwatering dessert for your loved ones.

Instructions: Melt the Chocolate: Chop the chocolate and melt it using a double boiler or in the microwave in short bursts. Stir until smooth. Allow it to cool slightly. Whip the Egg Whites: In a clean, grease-free bowl, beat the egg whites with a pinch of salt until soft peaks form. Gradually add sugar while continuing to beat until stiff peaks form. Prepare the Whipped Cream: In another bowl, whip the heavy cream until it forms soft peaks. Add the vanilla extract and mix gently. Combine the Mixture: Stir the egg yolks into the cooled melted chocolate until smooth. Gently fold in the whipped cream, followed by the beaten egg whites, a third at a time. Be careful not to deflate the mixture. Chill and Serve: Spoon the mousse into serving glasses or bowls. Refrigerate for at least 2 hours to set. Garnish and Enjoy: Top with whipped cream, shaved chocolate, or fresh berries before serving
